Jay’s Sandbox

Jay’s Sandbox is where I park experiments and half-finished ideas—usually maps, small tools, or visualizations that help me think through wind, land, and data. Nothing here is tied to a specific real-world project or employer. It’s all demos, dummy data, and explorations I’m comfortable sharing publicly.

Current highlight · Interactive webmap

Explore 40 Years of U.S. Wind Turbine Growth

This map shows every wind turbine in the United States and how the fleet has changed over time. Drag the time slider to watch turbines appear across the country as the wind industry grows. Each turbine is styled by its characteristics so you can see how designs have evolved over the last four decades.

It’s an easy way to explore when wind energy arrived in different regions, how the technology has scaled, and where the largest projects have been built.
