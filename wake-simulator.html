Model: simplified Jensen ("top-hat") wake — each rotor casts an expanding cone of slowed air downwind; overlapping deficits combine root-sum-square; power scales with wind speed cubed. Grid squares = 1 rotor diameter (D ≈ 163 m, a modern onshore machine). Real siting tools (Openwind, WindPRO) use the same physics with far more sophistication — this is the intuition, not the engineering.
